The Story Behind Quantum Shift

In the Quantum Shift universe, every correct answer nudges your ship a little farther along a purple trail of orbiting code. Variables, tags, and selectors all float around you like tiny stars waiting to be connected.

At lower levels, you are just stabilizing your orbit, learning how HTML tags fit together and how CSS selectors target them. At higher levels, your ship begins to “shift” across dimensions, where JavaScript starts wiring things together with logic and events.

There is no boss fight here—just a simple loop of trying, reading, and trying again until the concepts feel familiar.

Shift Levels

  • Warm-Up: Just getting used to basic syntax and terms.
  • Stable Orbit: You understand the main tags, selectors, and simple JS.
  • Quantum Jump: You can explain concepts to someone else in plain language.
  • Supernova: You’re ready to move on to larger projects and more complex logic.
